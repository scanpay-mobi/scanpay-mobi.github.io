Introducing ScanPay

Find convinience with SCANPAY

More often than never, we find ourselves in those long queues at the counter as we wait to purchase products across the supermarket counters. We came up with an innovative way to combact this challenge in the most convinient way possible. All a shopper has to do is to scan the product's barcode on his smartphone and pay for it using mobile money solutions.

Description

How it works

Scan

The shoper opens the app and scans the product's QR Code on his smartphone device.

Load Cart

Once the product has been scanned, Its details are detected on the application and The Products name and price are reflected on the user's Cart. For unwanted commodities, the User has an option to remove them from the cart

Check-Out

pay for the product on the app using a digital payment solution of your choice and Leave
